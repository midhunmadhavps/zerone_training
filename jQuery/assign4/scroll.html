Our Story

With Zerone beginning its journey as far back as 2004, .NET and open source development were our key areas of focus. In fact, it was also during these formative years that our very first projects in Data Analytics and IoT came to fruition too. In keeping up with the latest industry best practices, we adopted the Agile software development practice and which has been key in building process maturity over the years. Once we embraced organizational transformation from within, it was time to share our expertise and experience with those in need of our services and solutions. Since then, Zerone has forged ahead of the competition with an enviable client portfolio that has culminated in the delivery of more than five hundred projects. As a result, both NASSCOM and the Mirror Review have recognised our contribution to the broader digital transformation mantra that businesses have embraced and implemented across the board. Some of our innovative solutions used in development include .NET, J2EE, J2ME, WordPress CMS, Alfresco, Magento, Python, MERN and MEAN Stack etc. along the way.

Culture & Values

We Zeronites live by our core values. Commitment to our clients is paramount, thereby inducing a very customer-centric culture. We encourage our people to go the extra mile for our customers and be passionate at what they do. In doing so, we not only adhere to uncompromising degrees of honesty and ethicality, but also foster an environment of continuous learning and personal enrichment. We take our work seriously. However, we have our fair share of fun too. We hire people for their diversity in ideas and thoughts that in turn makes our work, fun and interesting. We take pride in being there in our professional and personal capacities, when needed most for our clients, partners and colleagues, unconditionally.
